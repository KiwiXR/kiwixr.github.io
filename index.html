About Me

  • I am a first-year master's student at the Transfer Learning Group (BIT-DA) at Beijing Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Shuang Li and Prof. Chi Harold Liu.

  • I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from BIT (2018-2022).

  • My research interests are in transfer learning and data-efficient scene understanding. My current research directions include domain adaptation for dense prediction tasks like semantic segmentation.
